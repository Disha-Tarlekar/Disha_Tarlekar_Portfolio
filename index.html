RetainIQ — Customer Churn Intelligence Dashboard
SQL · Python · Power BI · DAX

A 4-page Power BI churn intelligence system for a B2B SaaS business — built to quantify revenue at risk and give leadership actionable retention strategies with rupee estimates.

  • Cleaned 7,500 B2B SaaS records in MySQL — resolved nulls in NPS & support fields, enforced full data hygiene
